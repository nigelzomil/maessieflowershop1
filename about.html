Our Story

Who are we? Well, we're Maessie's, I suppose. What's there to know?

Nestled in the heart of Dangwa, the floral capital of the Philippines, Maessie’s Flowershop has been a haven for beauty and artistry since its founding in 2003. Our story began with a simple yet profound love for flowers and the emotions they convey. Over two decades later, that passion continues to bloom in every arrangement we create.

With a deep respect for nature, we are proudly engaged in environment-friendly practices, supporting local growers for fresh bloom procurement while benefiting the local community. So whether you are looking for an exquisite little bouquet that will bring joy or a wonderful arrangement for life’s great milestones, Maessie’s Flowershop will provide elegance and care in facilitating your vision.

Come into our shop and experience flowers that speak the language of the heart. At Maessie’s Flowershop, every arrangement is more than just a gift — it is a memory in bloom.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh and beautiful flowers sourced from the best growers.
  • Expert florists with years of experience in floral design.
  • Affordable prices and customizable bouquets.
  • Reliable and timely delivery services.
